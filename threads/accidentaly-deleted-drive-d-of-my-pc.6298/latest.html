You have lost a partition or whole HDD drive ?
If you lost a partition then It must be NTFS and if you lost whole HDD it must be NTFS too, cause only memory cards and pen drives uses FAT now a days.

Download Get Data Back for NTFS. You can search for full version on any P2P site.
Run the software, activate through given methods.
Open, select the cause of your data loss.
Choose : Systematical Damage Fdisk or Format OPTION
Click Next and you will get a list of drives and partiton,
If you have lost a partition(Drive D: C: E: ..etc) then select that partition or, if you have lost your HDD(HDD1 or HDD2) select that HDD.
Select scan tab, and let it scan your sectors ...
After Scanning, it will show you a list, choose accordingly and click next.
Select your files, you want to recover and export them.. kinda..
