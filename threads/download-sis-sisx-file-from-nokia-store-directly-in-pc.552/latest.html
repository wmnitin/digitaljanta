There are Certain Thing You have to care about ---
  1. You Must Logged in With Your Nokia Account
  2. The File You are Downloading Must be free (It Should not paid File)
  3. You Must have selected you phone
If You have Done with Above mentioned points , then you are ready to Download from Nokia Store ..

What You need to Do

  • Open File page You Want to Download ("and Check the url bar")
  • Replace ("http://store.ovi.com/content/55735?clickSource=homepage&pos=6") with ("http://store.ovi.com/content/55735/download")
