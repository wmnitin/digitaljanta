Lenovo showcased its three mid-range smartphones – the K6, K6 Power, and K6 Note. All the three devices are metal-clad with full HD 1080p displays. Powering these devices is the Snapdragon 430 chipset with Android 6.0 Marshmallow as the OS

The three devices will go on sale this month in Dark Gray, Gold, and Silver colors. Lenovo has not revealed the pricing for these devices. The K6 comes with 2GB of RAM, the Power comes in 2GB/3GB RAM variants, while the K6 Note comes in 3GB/4GB RAM options. All the three smartphones have microSD card slot for storage expansion

Lenovo K6 Specifications
5-inch Full HD IPS display with a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels
64-bit Octa-Core Qualcomm Snapdragon 430 chipset with Adreno 505 GPU
2GB RAM with 16GB/32GB expandable internal storage
Android 6.0 Marshmallow
13MP rear camera with LED Flash and 8MP front-facing camera
dual-SIM (optional), 4G LTE, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 4.1, GPS, Fingerprint sensor
3000mAh battery

Lenovo K6 Power Specifications
5-inch Full HD IPS display with a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels
64-bit Octa-Core Qualcomm Snapdragon 430 chipset with Adreno 505 GPU
Variants - 2GB RAM/16GB storage; 3GB RAM/32GB storage (expandable)
Android 6.0 Marshmallow
13MP rear camera with LED Flash and 8MP front-facing camera
dual-SIM (optional), 4G LTE, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 4.1, GPS, Fingerprint sensor
4000mAh battery

Lenovo K6 Note Specifications
5.5-inch Full HD IPS display with a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels
64-bit Octa-Core Qualcomm Snapdragon 430 chipset with Adreno 505 GPU
Variants - 3GB/4GB RAM with 32GB internal storage (expandable)
Android 6.0 Marshmallow
16MP rear camera with LED Flash and 8MP front-facing camera
dual-SIM (optional), 4G LTE, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 4.1, GPS, Fingerprint sensor
4000mAh battery​
