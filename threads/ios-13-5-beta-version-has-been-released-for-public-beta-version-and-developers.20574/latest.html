Hi Team,

iOS 13.5 API developed by Apple and Google for COVID-19 contact tracing. Contact tracing is not functioning right now however, as Goverment health authorities are planning to develop the applications to take advantage of the API.

There are two changes in iOS 13.5 based on COVID-19 situation.

1.Face ID can be detect if you are wearing a mask and skip directly to the passcode screen due to this you can easily unlock your phone.

2.New setting is FaceTime that allows you to disable the shifting design when you are using Group FaceTime calls. By default, the FaceTime app automatically enlarges and shrinks faces in a group call based on who’s talking, but this can be Irremovable more than a few people.

For stable and Final version will expected to be released by mid-May.
