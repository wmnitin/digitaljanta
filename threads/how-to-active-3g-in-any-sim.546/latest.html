How to Activate 3G on 2G SIM Card?
3G stands for high speed internet services , video calling services for GSM Mobile Phones , 2G users can switch to 3G Services by sending a sms , as per the list given below for respective operators,
Operator Name How to Activate 3G on 2G SIM Card
Airtel 2G & 3G Send “ACT 3G” on 121
AIRCEL 2G & 3G SEND”START 3G”ON 121
BSNL 2G & 3G SEND “ACT 3G” on 53733
Reliance 2G & 3G 3G ACTIVATE ON RECHARGE 351 or any tariff plan
Tata Docomo 2G & 3G SEND ‘ACT 3G” ON 121
Vodafone 2G & 3G
SEND ‘ACT 3G” ON 141
