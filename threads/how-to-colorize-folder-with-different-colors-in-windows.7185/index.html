Colorizing different Colors in your Folders :
Step 1. Download Folder Colorizer Software which is third party tool to assign color in your folder.
Step 2. Install Downloaded Folder Colorizer in your Pc.
Step 3. Navigate to the folder which you want to color.
Step 4. Now Right Click on your Desire Folder, Click on Colorize! > Click on your Color which you want.
Step 5. Now refresh and see the change.
Step 6. Repeat above steps for another Folder.
Step 7. Enjoy.

You may chose varius color by clicking on Colors How, Chose Folder > Right Click on Your Mouse > Colorizer > Colors.


You can restore with default color by going Colorize! > Restore Original Color.
