Bro u have to try to flash stock recovery via fastboot..
If ur android one is 1stgen.(with 4gb internal memory) than try these methods...
Important Notes.
A. Your device will be factory reseted so all of ur internal data,custom recovery,root will be gone after installing stock recovery.
B. If something goes wrong than don't blame me.

Steps.
1.Download stock rom for canvas A1.
Try this Here
2.Download and instal adb drivers
Click Here

3. Goto C:\adb ( adb drivers are installed here by default.)
4.Now extract the .zip file of ur stock rom into adb folder.
Place all the content like recovery.img, cache.img etc. in adb folder.
5.Open command prompt in adb folder.
press Shift+ryt. click inside that folder and select Open command window here.
5.Turn on USB Debugging in ur mobile.
6. Goto fastboot mode by turning off ur device and than press power+volume down key together .
7. Connect phone to pc.
8. Once in fastboot mode follow these commands carefully.

fastboot flash recovery recovery.img
fastboot flash boot boot.img
fastboot flash cache cache.img
fastboot flash userdata userdata.img
fastboot flash system system.img

When all done exit and restrart the device.
It will take around 5-10 minutes for 1st boot.

Watch this video Here
The video is for nexus device but it will make u understand better the final steps.
