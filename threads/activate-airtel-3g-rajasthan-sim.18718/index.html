Personally tested on Rajasthan Airtel Sim .

[PRO]

Follow steps :

1) Stop 3g (3G to 121) and select 2.
2) You will get message (3G deactivated )
3) Switch to 2G Mode
4) Activate 7Rs Pack (40MB 2g-3g-4g data) (I used Paytm)
5) Now call to CC and tell them to " Activate my 3G service"
6) Wait for a message (3G will be activated soon )
7) After 2 minutes switch to 3g mode and now you will see " 3G signal"
8) You can send message to 121 ( 3g to 121 )
9) You will get a sms " 3G service is already active on your Airtel." that means 3g successfully activated.
10 ) So now you have 40MB shared Data.
11) Now you know what to do

Note : I`m not using Airtel direct trick . I only created this method for testing . (firstly i had deactivated my 3g then reactivated by this method )
Don`t ask me about "Airtel Direct Trick " as i left this trick 3-4 months ago so now don`t have any idea about that !

[/PRO]
