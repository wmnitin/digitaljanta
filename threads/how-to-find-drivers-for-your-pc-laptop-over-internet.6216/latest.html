I
lost my motherboard cd and stuff and had no clue how to get drivers for
my pc as i formatted my pc. I searched and got to know to get my model
no. of system motherboard and search for the drivers over internet.
I

but unfortunately i was only getting “To be filled by O.E.M” everywhere i
was searching for the system no.
Note: One can check system model no. easily by following this step:
Just click START—> PROGRAMS—> ACCESSORIES—> SYSTEM TOOLS—> —> SYSTEM INFORMATION
If you are getting your system no. you can easily start googeling and search the drivers with model no.
And if you are getting “To be filled by O.E.M” message instead of model no. then you need to follow these steps:
Download >> Speccy software!

Speccy
will give you detailed statistics on every piece of hardware in your
computer. Including CPU, Motherboard, RAM, Graphics Cards, Hard Disks,
Optical Drives, Audio support. Additionally Speccy adds the temperatures
of your different components, so you can easily see if there’s a
problem!
