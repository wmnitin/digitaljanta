Fool google into thinking your phone as pixel, and it'll give u unltd storage..
[PRO][/PRO]Rooted devices only
Copy paste/replace this :-
In OEM.prop
ro.product.name=taimen
ro.product.manufacturer=Google
ro.product.model=Pixel 2 XL
ro.product.device=taimen
In Build.prop
ro.build.flavor=taimen-user
ro.product.brand=Google
ro.product.board=taimen
ro.build.product=taimen
ro.opa.eligible_device=true
Download nexus.xml from here - https://drive.google.com/file/d/0BwHacplF2a6CNHVmTUZDVnhxWEk/view
Open your root file manager of choice,
Copy the nexus.xml file you just downloaded and paste it in /system/etc/sysconfig
Set permission to rw-r-r
(i used es file explorer)
And reboot
If your phone doesn't have oem.prop then add/edit it's lines in build.prop itself
