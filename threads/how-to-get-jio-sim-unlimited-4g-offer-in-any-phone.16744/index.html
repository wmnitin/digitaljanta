If you remember before 2 days ago, @knox420 posted a thread regarding getting JIO sim without Lyf phone. Read here : http://www.digitaljanta.in/community/threads/trick-to-activate-reliance-jio-preview-offer-in-all-android-phones.16727/

Steps in brief:
1. Download myjio app from here https://drive.google.com/open?id=0BzGEoH0u2H0uR3hlcmhhWjludnM
2. Open myjio app & click install all jio apps
3. After installation of all apps, disconnect your internet & open myjio app, it will show a link to get JIO SIM.
4. If link has appeared, connect to internet & generate the CODE.
Click to expand...
So, I tested these steps & succesfully got the CODE that you need to show in jio stores to get JIO SIM.

I am pretty sure that most of you have generated the code.

After generating code, it will automatically saved in your gallery, to be safe take screenshot of code too.

Goto nearest JIO store (or Reliance digital xpress mini) :

1. Directly tell, I want JIO SIM, shop owner would tell you to buy a LYF phone.
2. Then tell, I have a code.
3. Take photocopy of address proof (I gave aadhar card), Identity proof (I gave pan card) & one passport size photo.
4. No need to show anything else like phone, bill etc. Just tell them "I have generated this code from my Samsung galaxy S6" (only if they ask).
5. You got your sim.

Now, wait for 24-48 hours to get your SIM active. After receiving activation SMS on your alternate number, put your JIO sim in same phone which you have used to generate code earlier.

Got your SIM activated ?
Your wait for unlimited 4G is still not over After SIM activation you will get only
2GB data for 3months
100 minutes for 3months

What ? where the hell is my unlimited preview offer. Don't worry aim of this thread is to tell you all steps.

Activating unlimited access :
After getting your sim activated, Goto https://www.jio.com & Signup using jio number. After completing signup. Open myjio app from phone again & login. On first screen you will see a link to submit. Click that submit link & your 3 months unlimited preview offer has been activated.

Till all these steps, dont uninstall any jio app from your phone
