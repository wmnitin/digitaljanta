Imagine, you’re in an obscure location and all that you have with you is a smartphone. In a such a situation, to you’re at a stop which is at a side of the road, seemingly in the middle of nowhere. Or else, think that you are in an imperative requirement of Wi-Fi but, you actually don’t have instant, secure access to the Internet. Also, your mobile has a limited data plan and you are in a critical situation that you need Wi-Fi right now. To overcome this issue, you need to find free Wi-Fi anywhere, anytime in the world no matter you have limited access to the Internet. If you’re a migrant who requires the internet all the time, then you need to find Wi-Fi hotspots obviously free Wi-Fi sounds more convincing. Well, we are here to guide you on how to find free Wi-Fi hotspots anywhere in the world. Have a look!
Simple Ways to Find Ubiquitous, Free Wi-Fi

We all know that, these days free, unrestricted Wi-Fi hotspots are popping up in many places throughout the world. Free Wi-Fi hotspots are essential while travelling as you won’t have your home Wi-Fi network and you actually don’t need to pay for international mobile data. Here, we are providing you simple ways to find free Wi-Fi hotspots anywhere in the world, whether you’re moving to a remote location or just to the other side of your home city.
1. Find a Restaurant Chain

When you visit a new location and you’re in the need of free Wi-Fi, then you may go to places where you have a free Wi-Fi. Are you in thinking, how to find a place where you will get free Wi-Fi? Don’t worry. Nowadays, most major cities are offering free Wi-Fi hotspots for the visitors.

FInd Free Wi-Fi Hotspots

For instance, Starbucks and McDonald’s are very popular restaurants that provide free Wi-Fi for its patrons. These two restaurants have a large number of places across the world that consistently provide free Wi-Fi. No matter about their coffee and food, free Wi-Fi which they provide is extremely good.
2. Some More Places with Free Wi-Fi

Wi-Fi has become most common phrase these days and many restaurants, coffee shops and cafes offer “free” Wi-Fi to its customers. There are many other places that offer free Wi-Fi that includes public libraries, public parks, etc. Even, community spaces like shopping malls, large department stores and bookstores provide free Wi-Fi for its customers. If you’re travelling to some new place and waiting for a bus to arrive, then you can use Wi-Fi in the public transportation hubs like airports, railway stations and bus stations.

Free Wi-Fi Spot

We already know that most popular place TajMahal is also offering free Wi-Fi for all the tourists and visitors. However, not all free Wi-Fi is completely free, because some of the independent coffee shops and restaurants may require you buy something before getting a login code. Choose the right location and get access for free Wi-Fi.
3. Locate Wi-Fi Hotspot using an App

We have a copious number of apps available for both Android and iOS that can help you in finding free Wi-Fi hotspots. To locate any Wi-Fi hotspot using an app, you need to have an Internet connection and for some other apps works well even if you’re offline. Here, we are listing few mobile apps for Android and iOS users that help you in finding free Wi-Fi hotspots anywhere in the world.
a. Wi-Fi Finder

WiFi Finder is an easy and aesthetically pleasing yet functional way to find Wi-Fi hotspots anywhere in the world. You can use this app if you’re seeking for a free Wi-Fi hotspot to reach on or some other paid public Wi-Fi hotspots. Once you install and run this app on your iOS or Android device, it immediately downloads a database of free and paid Wi-Fi hotspots around the world.

Wi-Fi Finder app for iOS and Android

Later, you can open this app anytime even without an internet connection and simply use it offline. This app will use your GPS location in order to show nearby free Wi-Fi hotspots on a map. If you’re a robust user of Skype, then Wi-Fi Finder is the best choice.

Pros

Locates tons of free and paid Wi-Fi hotspots. Allows you to look up for free Wi-Fi hotspots even when you’re offline. Filters to allow you to search for both free or paid hotspots. Filters the search by the type of provider and venue.
Cons

Provides offline database download. GPS location must be turned on to get the directions of Wi-Fi hotspot location. Two-paned view hides the map when they are both open.
Wi-Fi Finder for iOS and Android
b. Instabridge

Instabridge is a most popular app that assists you in finding and auto-connecting free wifi. The Instabridge app is available for both iOS and Android users. It is a Wi-Fi password-sharing community that auto-connects to nearby networks. Instabridge has a huge database of 3 million hotspots, but it is less when you visit abroad. You can connect to Wi-Fi spots that have been shared by other members of the community.

Instabridge app – Find Free Wi-Fi Hotspot

Once you download Instabridgeon your device, you can connect to a range of free wi-fi spots at any place and you can be in online for free. You can easily list your own home Wi-Fi and give access to your friends who send a request. The app is free and works in more than 200 countries.

Pros

Allows to get free Wi-Fi connections in all major cities. No data limitation and free of cost Once the connection is available, you can Auto-connect to Wi-Fi. Pre-map your next destination offline
Cons

Lack of social features that totally relies on interaction among the people. Need to fix some bugs and issues. Duplicate listings may be found that seems annoying.
Instabridge for iOS and Android
c. WeFi Pro

WeFi Pro is another useful app that helps you to find Wi-Fi hotspots anywhere in the world. It automatically connects your device to the best network in every location. It has two times faster data speeds that result in a faster video, sharper images, better gaming and many more.

WeFi Pro

This app has a built-in map that shows all Wi-Fi hotspots making it easier for the users to find the best connection. WeFi Pro has over 200 million hotspots that help you resist and slow networks and find the best WiFi anywhere in the world. WeFi Pro is available on Android devices.

WeFi Pro for Android

All these are the simple and possible ways to find free Wi-Fi hotspots anywhere, anytime across the world. We have mentioned most popular apps for Android and iOS that help you to find ubiquitous, free Wi-Fi. You can try to follow these simple ways to get free Wi-Fi whenever you travel or visit a new location.
