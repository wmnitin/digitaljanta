Ok. So this is a trick that I have personally discovered. It is being posted for the first time. And I beleive in posting things openly.

It is working in Punjab. Has been tested with 5 other friends of mine. Don't know about other states.

Simple steps:

Recharge with BBM Lite weekly plan (95 rs here). On the night of 6th day, keep the internet connected until its the 7th day. i.e you have to be connected from 11:59 Pm ( Day 6) to 12:01 am ( day 7). dial *123*6# and you may see this

"" .... and expiry date for this pack is 15 march . " ( Or any date according to your time)

Recharge again with the same plan in the morning. Done.

This procedure cause a glitch in BSNL servers. You will see that it is showing the 7th day as expiry date of plan ( *123*6#) even on the 8th and 9th day. Then on 9th day it will say -

" .... and expiry date for this pack is - . " which actually means "no date".

I have been using this trick from months now. The plan has not expired even after two months. And the speed capping got removed the very day it showed "-" as the expiry date. Previously speed was 60 , now its around 500.

Try at your own risk.

( If you are wondering why I posted about BBM speed problem earlier. I just wanted to check if anyone else had discovered this method. And I realized no one did. Or at least no one wants to share this openly. Wanted to post it earlier but could not come to the forums for some reason.)

Cheers!
