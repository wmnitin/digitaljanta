SupercrossPro. It is the first original motorcycle race, in which you can compete with the owners of devices on different platforms in on-line mode. Thus owners of Android and iOS devices may take part in competitions hosted on game server. You may play both on the tablet and smartphone. In Heat mode you compete one-on-one with your opponent. Before the race with your opponent you can try your hand in the Training mode. You will have an opportunity to upgrade your racer and the motorcycle.
