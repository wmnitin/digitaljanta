STEP ONE: CONNECT YOUR MAC/PC TO TWO OR MORE INTERNET CONNECTIONS.
You can connect you Mac/Pc to Internet through various ways it would be using WiFi, Ethernet port, USB etc.

STEP TWO: CHECK WHETHER YOU ARE CONNECTED TO TWO OR MORE WORKING INTERNET CONNECTIONS.
The screenshots below shows both Ethernet and Wi-Fi connected on Mac and PC respectively.


STEP THREE: COMBINE BOTH CONNECTIONS WITH SPEEDIFY.
Now that you have both Internet connections connected to your computer simultaneously, you might want to combine both networks for faster and more reliable Internet. This is where Speedify comes in. a particular app( any ) will use only one adapter ...
now the speedify app will choose the better connection of the 2 and will try that your connection is always connected and does not get dropped ...

BUT IT CAN DO THESE THINGS:- (AS PER MY EXPERIENCE ON CONNECTIFY DISPATCH )

1ST

  • DOWNLOAD FROM IDM USING ONE ADAPTER
  • AT THE SAME TIME BROWSE(CHROME OR MOZILLA OR ETC ) WITH ANOTHER ADAPTER
  • SO SPEED IS GOOD FOR BROWSING ALSO WHILE DOWNLOADING
2ND


  • DOWNLOAD FROM IDM USING ONE ADAPTER
  • AT THE SAME TIME DOWNLOAD FROM DIFFERENT DOWNLOAD MANAGER(EAGLEGET, FDM,ETC) WITH ANOTHER ADAPTER
SO IT DOES COMBINE TWO CONNECTIONS BUT IT DOES IT DIFFERENTLY AS I EXPLAINED ABOVE
