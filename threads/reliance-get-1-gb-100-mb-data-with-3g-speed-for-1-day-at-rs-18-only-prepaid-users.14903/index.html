Today we would like to share an amazing trick for our Reliance Prepaid users. Some of you might be knowing about this trick, but for those who don’t know, Dealnloot is here. With this trick, you can get 1GB + 100MB Data Balance which will run at 3G speeds. This data balance will be valid for 1 day. This is perfectly a safe trick with no sim-blocking issues. This pack will cost you Rs.18 only. So hurry up and avail this amazing trick now.

How to activate this pack?

1. Firstly, make sure you have atleast Rs.18 in your Main balance account.

2. Dial *129*2# from your Reliance number. This pack will cost you Rs.9 only.

Soon you will get a message from Reliance- “Recharge of Rs.9 is successful. Enjoy 1GB 2G Data. Valid for today.

3. After 5 minutes, dial *129*1# from your Reliance number. This pack will again cost you Rs.9 only.

Soon you will get a message from Reliance- “Recharge of Rs.9 is successful. Enjoy 100MB 3G Data. Valid for today.

4. Now send message MBAL to 55333 to check your Data Account balance.

Bingo, you got a total of 1GB+100MB in your data account at just Rs.18 only. You can now use this whole data balance in 3G speeds for 1 day. Enjoy.

Advantages of this trick-

→ Get full 3G speed upto 5Mbps or more.
→ Data will work in both Mobile and PCs/Laptops.
→ Download your favourite Movies, Songs, Applications, etc.
→ No speed issues.
→ No sim-blocking issues.

Points to Remember-

→ Activate the 2G pack first and then 3G pack at second. Otherwise you won’t get the 3G speed.
→ Keep a minimum of Rs.18 balance in your Main Account.
→ Pack will be valid for one day only. (12:00AM to 11:59PM)
→ May be this trick won’t work in your state. Working in Madhya Pradesh & Chattishgarh perfectly.​
