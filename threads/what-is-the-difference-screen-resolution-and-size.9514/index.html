Size is the physical measurement from top side to the bottom opposite side... it will be like 4 Inch or 5 Inch etc....

Screen Resolution means "Pixel Density per Inch".. The Higher the Resolution... The better.....

For Example.... on a 5 Inch Size Phone..... a 1280*720 will look clear and crisper than a 854*480 Resolution Phone
