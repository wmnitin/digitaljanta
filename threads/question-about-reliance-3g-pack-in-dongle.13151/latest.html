yes you can use that but there are few conditions

1. Put your sim in the dongle before recharging and use the whole data via the dongle only.
2. Donot put that sim back to the phone and then again back to the dongle, otherwise your data will lapse. Use it in the dongle only.
3. You can put that sim back to the phone once but if you will put it back to the dongle after putting in the phone your data will lapse.
