Description :

Sniper Deer hunting is the most realistic 3D hunting game with full of adventure in the most realistic environment. Hunt the deer with sniper gun and catch bonus to excel to the new levels. It’s the time to take out your sniper and hunt down deer by showing your sniper skills.
Sniper Deer hunting 3D mission level include:
Hunting 1: kill one deer to complete the mission
Hunting 2: kill at least two deer to complete the mission
Hunting 3: Kill three deer to complete mission 3

Purchase a new gun with the coins collected to accomplish further missions or you can buy the gun directly.
