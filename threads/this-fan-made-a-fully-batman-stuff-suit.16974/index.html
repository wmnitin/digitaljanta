most of us here areMarvel-DC fan. But somebody has crossed the limits and have made a Full suit of batman.

A guy from Ireland known as Julian Checkley has created an incredible real life Batman suit which comes with 23 functioning gadgets and will appear in the new Guinness World Records Gamer’s Edition 2017, out on 8 September.

The Irish guy Julian Checkley stated that “constructing a fully functional suit was not an easy task,” also added that “There were many long hours trying devising the gadgets and finding ways to store them on the suit. They are classic gadgets but also some are specific to Batman: Arkham Origins. I am immensely proud of my Guinness World Records title and to be part of the Gamer’s Edition is just the next level”.
