Hello Guys,

As you all must know that jio phone 1st pre booking has already over and most of you might have already booked that too. So now this post will clear all your doubts and questions.

What is Jio Phone?
This is a featured phone with 123 Keypad and runs on Kai OS. It supports 4g.

What is Kai OS?
Kai OS is very much different from android but similar to Java OS.

Will I be able to use other network 4G?
By default No, the phone support jio 4g but its unlocker will be soon available after few days.

I heard something about jio tv cable, what is this?
Ya you heard right jio tv cable is one of the thing which make the phone special, this 4g feature phone allows you to connect and cast your jio apps on your tv just by connecting the phone to tv via cable.

I have old CRT tv, will I be able to use jio cable?
Yes, JIO cable can even be connected to old crt tv.

Does it support both VOLTE AND VILTE?
Yes, you can do both voice and video hd lte calls using jio phone.

Is there any special pack for the phone?
Yes, you can recharge with 303 to also use jio cable for one month. Full details about this will be made available just in a few days.

I have booked but yet not received any further response from jio?
Don't worry, actually jio phone distribution is delayed by 10 days and will start from 10 SEPT now starting from major states like Delhi, Kolkata etc.

Why people is telling that the jio phone is free?
Yes if you return the phone along with the bill and your id proof used at the time of purchase after 3 years then you will get full refund amount hand to hand.

I like to use phone in my regional language, will I be able?
Most probably yes, Jio phone supports 22 regional languages.

Which JIO APPS it will support?
MyJio, JioMusic, JioTV, JioCinema, JioXpressNews, HelloJio, JioVideoCall, JioShare, JioGames
