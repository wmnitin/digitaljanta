Do you wanna share your VPN connection to other devices?

Now you can!

All you needed is:
- Rooted Phone (Important)
- Busybox Installed (Just google it)
- DF Tethering Fix(https://m.apkpure.com/df-tethering-fix/com.formichelli.tetheringfix)


Procedure:
1.) Download and Install DF Tethering Fix
2.) Connect to your VPN
3.) Open your Wi-Fi Tether / Wi-Fi Hotspot
4.) Open DF Tethering Fix
5.) Click Fix Tethering
6.) Grant as root
7.) [From other devices] Connect to your Wi-Fi
8.) And done.

Cautions:
You need to fix tethering every after reboot / after opening your tether/hotspot
