Requirements: Android 2.1 and up​
Overview: Send Free International Text Messages​
Now you can send free SMS to over 150 countries for free!​
​
​
​
Why use 24SMS?​
• Send free texts to any mobile device in more than 150 countries.​
• Your friends don’t need to have the app to receive your texts.​
• Can send free SMS from anywhere in the world where you have a data connection or WIFI.​
• Unicode (UCS-2) support,you can send SMS in many languages including Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Greek, and Russian.​
​
Really IMPORTANT:​
• 24SMS is one way text.You can only send SMS.​
• You still use your own number to receive SMS.​
• Friends need to use Android app or www.24SMS.net to reply.​
• We use Google App Engine servers,some countries block access to Google App Engine,24SMS may not work.​
• Requires OS 2.1+​
