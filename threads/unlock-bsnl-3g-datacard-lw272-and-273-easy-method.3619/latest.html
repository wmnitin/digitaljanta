Unlock BSNL 3G datacard: LW272 and 273​

This is the most easy way to unlock bsnl 3g datacard to make it work in any sim so just follow the steps:
  1. Click on "Start"
  2. Then click on "RUN" and in run dialog box write "regdit" without quotes
  3. Locate the following registry entry: HKEY_LOCAL_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Linktop3g
  4. click on it and on right side there will be a entry naming "engineer" double click on it and change the value from 0 to 1
  5. Press OK
You are done now.insert any sim into data card and make its connection profile and enjoy it.Hope all of you had understand it.
