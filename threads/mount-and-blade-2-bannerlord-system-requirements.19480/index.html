Mount and Blade 2 will require a mid range computer to run. Currently we expect an Intel i5-2400 or AMD FX-8120 processor to meet recommended requirements. The graphics card requirement for BannerLord will be around a GTX 750 ti or Radeon HD 7850 and 8GB system memory to get 1080p high graphics settings at 60 frames per second
Here are the Mount and Blade 2:
  • PU: Intel i3-2100 / AMD FX-6300
  • CPU SPEED: Info
  • RAM: 4 GB
  • VIDEO CARD: Intel HD 4600 / Nvidia GT730 / AMD R7 240
  • FREE DISK SPACE: 40 GB
