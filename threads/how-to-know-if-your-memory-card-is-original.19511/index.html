How to Know if Your Memory Card is Original
You will need to download an application called SD Insight, which is available on the Google Play Store. Click here to download.

By using SD Insight android application, you can easily find the information about your memory card. You will be quickly able to know the name of the manufacturer of the SD card, storage size, and the manufacturing date. By clicking on the Eye-Icon on the top, you can see the serial number and other details as well.

If you get an error message – SD card is invalid, then there might be an issue with your memory card, and you need to format and reinsert the memory card into your mobile device.

If you get a message – Origin is unknown, then its because the application don’t have the information about your SD card manufacturer.

SD Insight Android App (Official Description)
SD Insight is a free, fast and easy to use application that you use on your cell phone. Within seconds the application reads the technical details from your SD card and displays the information in an easy to understand format.
