Hey...As I Moved From...Delhi To Rajasthan For My Studies...
I Found A Working Trick...to Get 3G Speed In 2G Data Plan...For Both Airtel and Reliance Users Only

So What You Have To Do Is...Very Simple...
1.Activate Any 2G Data Plan On Your Sim.
2.Activate 3G On Your Sim By Sending a SMS " 3G " To 121.(Without The Quotes)
3..Change Your Network Mode To 3G OR HSPDA OR UMTS.(Only Doing This Step 3 For Reliance You Will Get 3G Speed In 2G Plan)
4.Enjoy The Power Of Airtel and Reliance 3G.
NOTE:->Like Earlier Tricks You Dont Need To Change The Network Mode Again and Again.
REMEMBER:-> If You Dont Get 3G Speed Then Activate The 3GVBCPlan.
