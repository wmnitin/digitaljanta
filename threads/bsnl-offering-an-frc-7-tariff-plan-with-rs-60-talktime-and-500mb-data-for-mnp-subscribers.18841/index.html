Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) is now welcoming the subscribers of Reliance Communications (RCom), Docomo, Telenor to join its network as the three operators will be soon shutting down their business. BSNL has introduced a first recharge plan of Rs. 7, which is valid for all the customers who’re looking to port into BSNL network.
The FRC 7 tariff plan from BSNL gives Rs. 60 talktime and 500MB data for 28 days. “Rs 7 FRC plan is open to all port-in-to-BSNL customers who want their earlier numbers to be retained but will have to wait for activation. We are giving them instantly activated SIM on which they can get Rs 60 talktime and 500MB data for merely Rs 7,” said BSNL GM (Marketing) Rameshwar Singh recently.
Do make a note that this plan is applicable only for BSNL port-in customers and not for customers who’re joining the network.
