#Flipkart launches it's first smartphone #Billion #Capture+ with dual camera running on Snapdragon 625 processor starting from Rs.10,999

Flipkart Billion Capture+ specifications

5.5-inch (1920 x 1080 pixels) IPS 2.5D display with Dragontrail glass protection
Hybrid Dual SIM (nano+nano/microSD)
Android 7.1.2 (Nougat), upgradable to Android 8.0 Oreo
2GHz Octa-Core Snapdragon 625 Mobile Platform with Adreno 506 GPU
3GB /4GB RAM
32/ 64GB storage, expandable memory up to 128GB with microSD
13MP (RGB) + 13MP (Monochrome) Dual rear cameras with Dual-tone LED flash
8MP front-facing camera
Fingerprint sensor
4G VoLTE, WiFi 802.11 b/g/n, Bluetooth 4.2, GPS, USB Type-C
3,500mA battery with Quick Charge
Colors: Mystic Black and Desert Gold
Price: Rs. 10,999 for the 3GB version and Rs. 12,999 for the 4GB version
Availability: November 15 from Flipkart
