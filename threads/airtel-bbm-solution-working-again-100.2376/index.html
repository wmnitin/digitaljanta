FOLLOW THESE STEPS

1. use a paid net / use an indian ip proxy.
2. disable idm for google chrome.
3. open chrome and open a incognito mode window (shift+ctrl+n).
4. open the fileice page where the file is there.
5. choose regular download option.
6. choose the option of FANCY.COM or SNAP DEAL SURVEY (if the option don't come up refresh the page)
7. the snap deal survey now need a phone no. for confirmation of the code
8. the fancy.com survey don't require a phone no. but 10minutemail mail will not be accepted (make a new gmail or yahoo account)
9. snap deal is better if you have not registered your phone no otherwise use fancy.com
10. register your mail address at fancy.com and choose a password then further selecting 2-3 options(they will ask you to choose their different products, select them ).
11. the downloading will start for sure.
12. it works for me on 10/10 occasions.
best of luck
