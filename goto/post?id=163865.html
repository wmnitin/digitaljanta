Hey Without waiting.. I am giving you the best method of removing shortcut virus form flash drive.

Process --
* Open CMD (Command Prompt)
* now copy paste this code-- attrib -h -r -s /s /d D:\*.*
Note:-- Here " D " is you flash drive letter. So you must change this With your Drive letter.

Hit ENTER after pasting this code. Enjoy... Now the shortcut virus has gone..
