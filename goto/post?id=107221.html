Pelecoin's emission is based on a simple proprietary algorithm, accessible and clear to all internet users. The system generates pelecoins on every event, increasing currency's (pelecoin) worth:

  • new user registration
  • acceptance of pelecoin for real goods or services
  • trade between pelecoins and real currencies (USD, Euro …) on the internet currency exchanges
The initial distribution of newly generated coins is based on user's participation in an event. The emission and the distribution use simple rules, allowing unexperienced users to actively participate in the process and to achieve significant amounts of coins in a short period of time. Instead of complicated (and recondite) search for crypto configurations proposed by other crypto currency systems, Pelecoin automatically increases your account when one of the users referred by you creates an account or pays with pelecoin for real goods or services.
