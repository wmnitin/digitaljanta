Steps To Follow :

[PRO]
1) Recharge with unlimited 2G plan

2) Activate a 3G plan
3) Now use normally
4) When balance reach to 10mb then u will get a message about that and afterwards u will be get 2G speed !
5) Now simply add a number to family share And speed will be back !
6) Now use slowly net !
7) And stop using net When net is 1mb and again connect and open google on uc and then check balance (Repeat this step untill u see u have only 0.20mb in main ac)(To check dial *121*2# Or *129*08# )
8) When balance reached to 0.20mb then disconnect net and put into flight mode and wait 30sec and turn it off and if u see U are getting network (3G) then u are lucky otherwise just deactivate it by (3G to 121 And STOP 3G) And reactivate by dialing to customer care (198 Or 121)
9) After 3G activated Now restrict backgroud data***(Must) And then connect net !
10) Now open uc and open uc and use 0.05mb and u will be see 0.15mb will be there in ur ac and also that time disconnect net and u will be get share data balance exhausted message but dont worry As check balance and there u will see 0.15mb is still there !
11) Now again connect net and restrict background data and Do ADD-REMOVE-ADD Family share 2times and at last mae sure family share activated !
12) Thats it ! Enjoy unlimited !
Notes :
1) Disable all news,cricket,notification in uc otherwise u cant use 0.05mb as news,cricket will take all ur data at a glance !
2) Force stop all running apps which can use net !
3) Use lite mode in uc to use 0.05mb in a session !
4) U cant excuse me if u cant use 0.05mb in a session and if u cant activate 3G by 198 Or 121 !
