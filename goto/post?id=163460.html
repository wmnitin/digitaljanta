AS YOU ALL KNOW THAT SOME PEOPLE ARE FACING SPEED ISSUE IN JIO AND TO SOLVE THIS ISSUE THEY ARE TRYING TO GET INTO THE BAND 40 OF JIO WHICH IS 2300Mhz band and is known for good speed...


MANY SUGGEST TO INSTALL MTK ENGINEERING MODE AND CHANGE THE BAND
BUT YOU KNOW THAT THIS DOESN'T WORK FOR SNAPDRAGON DEVICES...ONLY MEDIATEK DEVICES ARE SUPPORTED BY THAT APP...

SO
FOR THE SNAPDRAGON DEVICES
HERE IS THE PERFECT SOLUTION

JUST DIAL THIS USSD IN THE DIALER

*#*#83781#*#*
AND SEE THE MAGIC


NOW
*GO TO NETWORK MODE
*Select TD-LTE for Band 40 (2300MHZ)
*FDD-LTE for 1800/850 MHZ
*If you want to use both select TD-LTE, FDD-LTE

TESTED ON LYF FLAME 6
