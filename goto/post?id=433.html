This is the First hand Review of Galaxy S4.I have been using this phone for last 6 months and still everyday it does something to surprise me.
Samsung Galaxy S5 pre-booking has been started by Samsung and this is the time when they will slash S4 prices.I will be sharing my top 5 favourite reasons that might force you to buy this phone....

  1. It's flawless.There has never been a moment in last 6 months that it got hanged or stopped working....but you must expect that from top end phone from Samsung...
  2. It's complete.Every game that you have heard of till now.......will run....
  3. It's design.Even though the screen is 5 inches.... your thumb will be able to touch every corner of the phone.
  4. Stunning Screen.Full HD(1920X1080) packed into a 5 inch screen looks brilliant.Watching videos on S4 will drop your jaws.
  5. Great Camera.13Mp Camera takes picture as crisp as it can get....
It also contains additional features like air gestures ,smart scroll and multi-window but it did not feature in my top 5 because even though they will have an awe effect but it is not something you will use on daily bases....
