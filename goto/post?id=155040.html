1) You might end up with this warning on some websites.. so incase you want to remove it.. you have to disable adblock for this specific website alone..

2) Look for your browser's extension list.. click on adblock extension.. it would be enabled by default.

3) Click "Enabled on this site" once.. it would be disabled..



4) Reload the page/Website.. You are Done !! ​
