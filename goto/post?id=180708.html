​
BSNL's new Triple Ace plan for mobile customers is priced at Rs. 333 offers unlimited data with 3G speed up to 3GB/day which'll remain valid for 90 days.

This means you can enjoy a total of 270GB high speed 3G data at Rs. 333, or just Rs. 1.23 per GB.
Notably, BSNL customers on this plan will be limited to 3G speeds, compared to 4G offered by the likes of Reliance Jio, Airtel, Idea, and Vodafone. Still, it’s a pretty sweet deal if you the BSNL 3G data speeds in your region are decent enough...

Once an user exhausts 3GB of data, the speed will get reduced to 80kbps for the rest of the day.
In this BSNL 333 Plan , calls are Chargeable.

BSNL also announced unlimited 3G plan 1099 which is truly UNLIMITED in terms of speed & data usage..
