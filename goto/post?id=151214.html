Aircel’s ‘More than data’ packs start at Rs. 76 that offer 400MB 3G data for 14 days. There is another pack of Rs. 155 that offers 1GB 3G data for 28 days. For Rs. 298 pack, the customers will get 2GB 3G data for 28 days. In all these three packs, STD calls are at 40p/min.

Price Data Benefits Validity Call Rates Easy Activation code

Rs 76 400 Mb 3G data 14 days All Local & STD calls at 40p/minute *121*076#
Rs 155 1 GB 3G data 28 days *121*1550#
Rs 298 2 GB 3G data 28 days *121*298#

#For Aircel Mumbai customers only.

Sources: TT
