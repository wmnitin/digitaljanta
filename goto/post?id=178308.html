How To Send money with Gmail

  1. Open Gmail Or Gmail App
  2. Sign Up or Sign in with Gmail account.
  3. The Click the Compose button.
  4. Enter the email address of the person you want to send money to.
  5. Add a subject and message text (optional).
  6. Click the $ icon (£ for UK users).
  7. Enter the amount you want to send and choose a payment method or add a new one.
  8. Click Review.
  9. Click Attach.
  10. Check over your message one more time and click Send.
Receive an email with money attached

The first time you receive money through Gmail, you’ll need to add a debit card to transfer the money to Users. Whenever someone sends you money through Gmail, it will automatically transfer to that card.

To claim money sent to you through Gmail the first time:

  1. Open Gmail or Gmail App.
  2. Open the message with attached money.
  3. Click Claim money. As Mentioned in the below Gif Image.
