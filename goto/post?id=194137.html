Hello friends,
All of you who are jio users might have noticed that there is a decrease in the speed of jio network in past few days. Many of you may also had complained the customer care about this but still there is no effect even then.
So, I am here with all the details and solution of this problem.

Why is this happening?
The main reason behind this decrement in speed is the number of users that jio is gaining day by day. The company is fulfilling the quantity of users as per their business plan but deteriorating the quality provided to those users. Recently after the distribution of jio phone started the number of jio users has increased drastically and so as the load on each of the towers. This has led to lower bandwidth being provided to each one of the users. So due to overload on all towers the speed and quality has decreased.

So, now what is the solution?
The solution to this problem is very simple, and jio is also working on it as per latest news and updates. The company plans to plant over 50000 new towers in India and also increase the capacity to transmitters on each of the tower. But this is not just a work in a pinch. This is a long process and will definitely require 1 to 2 months.

What we must do?
Since JIO is not providing quality in many regions of India so we must PORT to other network or switch to other networks. If the number of users on jio is decreased then automatically rest of the users will get speed and quality. Also you may allow or contact jio network team for planting tower on your home or land. This will increase the speed near your home and also they will pay you a plenty for that.

Summary:-
1. After jiophone distribution, jio speed and quality has reduced.
2. No instant solution to this problem exists.
3. Better is to switch to other network if your data pack is over.
