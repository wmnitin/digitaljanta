Both of these can be charged from any level, You don't have to fully discharge them before charging. These kinds of batteries can have a problem of low voltage and it can suck your battery life. So what we can do to solve this issue? Using a Battery saver or a Battery doctor software can actually work?

Few Tricks
1- to increase your smartphones battery life don't fully charge it all the time. Unplug your charger before the battery level of 100%. It can do the trick for you and your battery will be increased.

2- Always start charging from 20%. Don't fully empty your battery. That can also work for you.
3- If your phone has an AMOLED screen use a black screen wallpaper to increase your battery time.
