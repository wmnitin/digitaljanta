I am sharing few Tips and Tweaks related with Reliance 3G Proxy Trick.

NOTE : These Tips and Tweaks are tested privately on my system and found to be effective, So if in any case, they don't work for you then I am not responsible for wasting your own time and energy. Also please note down all the changes that you perform in your system, so that if anything goes wrong then you can undo the changes.

Best time for Downloading Content
Click to expand...

Speed is at best during 01:00 AM - 08:00 AM (As per my experience)[/HIDE]

[HIDE=10]
Best Host for Optional Speed
Click to expand...
(Recommended)

For finding out which host giving best speed in your area, first you will need to disconnect your modem -> Disable Proxycap / Proxifier, then establish the connection again and now ping each host one by one. Check which one gives lower ping in your network, that host is best for you.

For me, the host are in the following order >>

31.13.95.8:80
31.13.95.246:80
199.16.156.200:80
199.16.158.181:80[/HIDE]

[HIDE=15]
Reliance Speed Booster
Click to expand...
(Recommended)

Disconnect your Modem -> Disable Proxycap / Proxifier -> Change APN from rcomwap / smartwap to rcomnet -> Connect your Modem -> Surf /Download approx 2-3 MB -> Now Disconnect your Modem -> Change APN from rcomnet to rcomwap / smartwap -> Enble Proxycap / Proxifier -> Connect your Modem again.

NOTE : This Boost Tip won't work if you are by default not getting speed in rcomnet APN, else this quick boost should definitely enhance the speed in Reliance Proxy Trick.[/HIDE]

[HIDE=15]
How to minimize Deduction Issues
Click to expand...
(Recommended)

Simply after connecting Modem, the first thing you need to do is re-download any small file added in your IDM (it can be a small .apk file, .mp3 file, any small software etc...) As soon as download finishes, Disconnect your modem, Check your data balance by dialing USSD *111*1*3# and Confirm that deduction happened or not, If deduction is minor as compared to what you have downloaded then Proxy is working and you can continue for the next 30 minute then After 30 minutes, Redo the whole procedure.

[HIDE=15]
Increase Data Quota
Click to expand...
(Recommended)

Dial *129# USSD Code -> Goto 9 Rs Menu -> Activate 9 Rs 3G Pack -> This data will be add up into your Main 3G data pack and Validity will remain same.

NOTE : This Data Tip won't work if you don't have SIM of a state where Reliance have its own 3G services.[/HIDE]
