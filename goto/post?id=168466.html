  4. add number
  5. consume 3g data till you are left under 5 mb
  6. now do RA RA RA RA RA RA
  7. download just 1 mb file and disconnect
  8. remove share
  9. change network to gsm
  10. now slowly slowly consume data till you reach under 1 mb
  11. now do connect disconnect connect

  16. activate 3g by app or customer care etc...if not success then simply do sp trick to get back 3g signal
