Let me quickly dash into the main
thing guys. Troid vpn is a new vpn for
Android users; works like droid but different
from droid vpn.
It allows you to use 1GB free data per day.
Unlike droid which only permit 100mb for
free account, troid permit 1GB free data
daily.
Benefits of Using TroidVpn
==>1GB free data daily
==>Fast in connection
==>No registration required
==>Allow you to connect to 8 free servers
from 5 locations (UK , USA , DE , NL , CA)
==>It is free for use.
==>Rooting is not required
Can it work on all Android Device?
It works perfectly on all android device with
minimum version of 4.0.4 and above.
How Can I Get Troid VPN
==>Visit Google play download it or download
at troidvpn.com
==>Run and install it on your device
==>Set it to UDP port 500
==>Connect and enjoy the speed.
This TroidVpn is new in Android market, fast
and secured, bypass restricted i.p addresses
and works both on unrooted or rooted
android devices including Tab 3.
