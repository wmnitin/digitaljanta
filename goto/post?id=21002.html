AS THIS TRICK IS AMOUNT TAKING SO PLZ TRY IT AT YOUR OWN RISK
First Step:
1. Make sure there is no active 2G data Plan.
2. Activate 3G on your Docomo. ACT 3G to 53333
3. Your Cell should show 3G, network

Second Step:
4. Dial *141#
5. and from the menu select, Internet Packs and select One time activation
6. Select the Tariff you like from Rs. 5 to Rs. 95 and Done

The Trick only works, when you activate 2G pack from your cell phone. Make sure you do not recharge from the Dealer, hence the trick will not work.

The 3G should be active, for the trick to work (before the 2G recharge). Try with Rs. 5 pack and reply back
