*How to download paid apps from playstore*

Requirements

1. GetApk Market

2. Your Device

3. A functional playstore

Procedure

1. Download getapk market.

2. Install it on your device.

3. Open PlayStore and navigate to the Paid Apps Section

4. Select an app.

5. Scroll down to the Share section and select share with GetApk

6. Continue to open with GetApk

7. Upon availability confirmation, select the get apk prompt. Next select the server with the latest version of the app.

8. Select your preferred option of either continuing the process through the embedded browser or open it through your custom browser.

9. Download the app.

Download link for the getapk app
http://download.techylist.com/getapk-market.1.6.94.apk
