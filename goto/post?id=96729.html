I am going to share a VLC trick by which you can convert your PC or Mobile videos using VLC.

How to Convert Videos Format Using VLC Media Player?
  • First of all Open VLC Player.
  • Now Go to Media > Convert/Save and Click on Convert/Save or simply Press ctrl+R
  • Now Click on Add and add Video from your desire location than click on Convert/Save.
  • Now select your desire format in which format you want to Convert and than click on Start.
  • Done
