Note: I'm sharing What I faced .....and was able to make three times this trick works from last 3 months....and currently using this trick w/o any problem

My Experiment :
[PRO]
After Every Speed cap I always leave my 3g status in shared mode for 1-2 hours and then give another try

I tried This 3-4 days ago, and works in very first try
1) current 3g status : shared mode ( 0 mb speed capped)
2) remove fam share
3) Rc with 27 Rs 3g pack
4) Now connect and starts consuming data
5) in my case after every rc w/o fam share my data stuck at some mb like 30.08...
6) after 2-3 hours of consuming data, will get 10 mb left msg and speed will cap
7) Now DC
8) A-R-A instantly....after that you will get exhausted msg and speed will cap again
(Now-a-days you will not get any exhausted msg but speed will cap)
9) Remove and wait for 1 min and then add fam share , speed will back
10 ) Now connect starts consuming data....data will stuck again at 0.24 mb
11) Now data starts reducing...like 0.2 mb....and so on
12) speed will cap again, and their will be two possibility,
a) leaving some 3g data like 0.1 or 0.05 ( don't DC, Use your brain and trick will work )
(in my case , left with 0.1 mb and made to work)
b) leaving 0 mb 3g data ( DC and try again after 1-2 hr)


Other way to use this trick ( used all time before ) :

a) Start this at 7-8 PM
b) follow 1) to 4)
c) you will get 10 mb left msg before 12 AM, DC and don't use in b/w 12 am to 6 am
d) now follow 8) to 12) after 6 AM
Benefit is in one rc you will get 2-3 hours in night before 10 mb left and after morning speed will cap at 3-6 pm


There is only one step to do after getting 0.05 or 0.1 mb,
use your brain...

Some Important Tips :

After setting up trick, leave your 3g status as it is, don't change it or you will face 12 or 6 am speed cap problem

If Signal lost don't use SP, wait for 5-10 min in airplane mode, or rc with any 9 Rs 2g/3g pack (not added in main 3g bal )

UPDATE:
After 0.05mb left....don't DC just to R-wait-A you will get back speed , after that you may DC, your 3g bal goes 0 mb

[/PRO]

Working without any problem from last 3-4 days ...no speed cap, no signal lost
