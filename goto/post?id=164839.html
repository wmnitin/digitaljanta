List of Devices that are eligible for Reliance Jio Preview Offer !
​

Hi DigitalJanta,
As everyone knows previously Reliance Jio Preview offer were available for All LYF and few Samsung S series devices, But now Jio has extended it's Preview Offer for few more Samsung, Asus, Panasonic, Yu, Micromax and LG Devices...etc. [check below for detail list devices which are eligible for Jio Unlimited Preview offer ]

From today, People using Samsung, Asus, Panasonic, Yu, Micromax & LG etc. smartphones can visit a nearby Reliance Digital or Xpress Mini Store to get their Jio SIM card and previously Jio were selling Prepaid sims only but now Peoples can select between a Prepaid & Postpaid SIM as per your requirement.
First check whether your device is eligible for preview offer or not (check device list below) if yes the follow further steps :-
Step 1: Click here and Download My Jio app
Step 2: Open My Jio app and wait till Get Sim option appears and then click.
Step 3: Then follow simple instructions to generate an Offer Code.
Step 4: Visit Reliance Digital / Dx Mini store / select modern trade outlets with
1) Original Proof of Address (POA) and copy,
2) Proof of Identity (POI) and copy
3) One passport size photograph.
Please ensure you carry your Jio Preview Offer Code along with the handset.
  • Customers would get unlimited 4G data, SMS, voice and video calls for 90 days at zero cost.
  • They would get exclusive benefits like access to Jio premium apps Like Jio Premium apps like JioPlay, JioOnDemand, JioBeats, JioMags, JioXpressNews, JioDrive JioSecurity and JioMoney etc.
