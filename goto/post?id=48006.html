The new features of Nokia Amber update for
Windows 8 mobile operating system are Smart
camera, Glance screen, Flip for silencing the phone ,
FM radio and Data sense app. The update will also
ring Here Maps v3.4, Here Transit v3.5 and Here
Drive+ v2.2 apps. Other app suites updates are Here
Location and Commerce app.
Nokia Amber update for Windows 8 mobile
operating system will be available for Lumia 520,
620, 720, 820 and Lumia 920. The Amber update is
the official Windows Phone 8 GDR2 update. All the
WP8 smartphones will get this update in this year.
Consumers can also update their MyNokiaBlog in a
simple method. But the update will wipe the
information on the device. The update can be
installed by using the Nokia Software updater .
