Nokia Lumia 625 and Lumia 925 launched in India at Rs 19,499 and Rs 33,499:-




With many of Nokia Lumia 625 features similar to recently launched Lumia 1020 ,Nokia Lumia 625 runs on Windows Phone 8 and powered by 1.2GHz, Dual Core processor. It sports 5 MP Autofocus, LED flash,1080 pixels @30fps, VGA front facing camera and 512 MB RAM, 8 GB with support for up to 64 GB SD card.
The user can have access to more than 165,000 apps including Vimeo, Temple Run and WhatsApp. The company claims that Lumia 625 offers faster mobile fun, as well as safer surfing with Internet Explorer 10 - making it ideal for viewing videos, games, and other content.
http://www.telecomtiger.com/download.aspx?ppt_id1=95&type=16
Nokia Lumia 625 will be available in a range of colours including orange, yellow, bright green, white and black with an array of changeable shells enabling easy personalisation.
The 4.5- incher Nokia Lumia 925 has Operating system Windows Phone 8 and is powered by 1.5 GHz dual-core Snapdragon. As far as display is concerned it has 4.5 inch AMOLED WXGA (1280 x 768), 2.25 D sculpted Gorilla 2 Glass, ClearBlack, High Brightness Mode, Sunlight Readability, Super Sensitive Touch for glove and nail usage.
http://www.telecomtiger.com/download.aspx?ppt_id1=94&type=16
It has PureView 8.7 megapixels main and 1.2 megapixels wide angle front camera. It comes with 1 GB RAM, 16 GB internal memory with 7 GB free SkyDrive cloud storage. It has 2000 mAh battery, Wireless Charging supported via an accessory cover.
Nokia Lumia 625 Features:
• 4.7 Inch (11.94 cm) Touchscreen
• Windows Phone 8 OS
• 1.2GHz Dual Core Qualcomm Snapdragon S4
• 5 MP Rear Camera & VGA Front Camera
• 3G
• Wi-Fi
• GPRS/EDGE
• Bluetooth
• 8GB Internal Memory
• Expandable Memory Up to 64 GB
• FM Radio
• Music Player
• Video Player
• 3.5mm Audio Jack
Nokia Lumia 925 Key Features:-
• Windows Phone 8
• 1.5GHz Dual Core processor
• 4.5inch Pure Motion HD+ screen
• 8.7MP Pure View camera
• 16GB Mass Memory and 1 GB RAM
• 2000mAh powerful battery
