Samsung has unveiled a new Android Budget Phone - The Samsung Galaxy Trend. A nice phone with capable features. Check it out and comment if you like this one from the stable of the smartphone giant.




The key features:

  • Android (Jelly Bean) OS
  • 1 GHz Processor
  • Dual SIM (GSM + GSM)
  • 3 MP Primary Camera
  • FM Radio
  • 4-inch TFT Capacitive Touchscreen
  • Wi-Fi Enabled
  • Expandable Storage Capacity of 32 GB
