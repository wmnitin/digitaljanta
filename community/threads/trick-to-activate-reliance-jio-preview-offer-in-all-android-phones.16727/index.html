Trick To Activate Reliance Jio Preview Offer In All Android Phones

1. Download MyJio app from https://goo.gl/EwzFES

2. Once you download, open it and click on " Install All " button

3. Now wait for few minutes &amp; let it download all 10 apps of MyJio app.

4. Now fully close MyJio app.

Here Comes The Tricky Part....

5. Once you download all apps, Disconnect your Internet connection whether its Wifi or Mobile Data.


6. Now open MyJio app ( Do NOT turn ON Internet connection)

7. Once you open MyJio app, click on Open just beside MyJio

8.&nbsp;Once you click on Open, you can see Get Jio Sim option.

9. Boom !! Now turn ON your Data and fill all required details.

10. Finally visit your Nearest Reliance Jio Store and show them your Barcode for Jio Sim

Note : This offer is not officially launched yet for all phone so Reliance agents might refuse. So if you have convincing power then try to convince them and grab your Jio Sim
